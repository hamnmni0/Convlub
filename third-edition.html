The Optimist x ConvLUB

We just wrapped up the 3rd edition of CONVLUB x The Optimist, and the energy in the room was unreal.

Over 30 people showed up from Poland, Italy, Georgia, Kenya, Ukraine, and beyond for a night that felt bigger than trivia and stronger than a typical night out.

It became a space where people met, connected, laughed, and built real friendships. Winners took the trivia crown, and it all happened over coffee, matcha, and cakes.
