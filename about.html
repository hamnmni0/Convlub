About ConvLUB

A community space built around belonging, exchange, and local connection.

ConvLUB was founded on the belief that together we are stronger. It is a space designed for people to meet, connect, and feel like they truly belong.

What ConvLUB stands for

By hosting international-style events in collaboration with local businesses, ConvLUB brings people from all walks of life together while also bridging cultures.

It is especially focused on helping the international community feel closer to Polish culture, while giving local participants the chance to meet new perspectives, stories, and traditions in a natural way.

Why it matters in Lublin

Cities grow stronger when people feel seen, welcomed, and invited into shared experiences. ConvLUB is built around that idea: not only events, but environments where conversation feels easy and strangers can turn into community.

Every edition is designed to feel warm, social, and open, whether the evening is centered on conversation, music, talent, or simple moments of connection.

How ConvLUB grows

The project grows through collaboration, recurring editions, and a lasting archive of what each event felt like. The goal is not only to host memorable nights, but to document them and keep building a story people want to return to.

ConvLUB is where different worlds meet, share, and grow together.

ConvLUB builds spaces where people meet, connect, and belong.
